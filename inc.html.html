About Comrades Revolution Alliance

Comrades Revolution Alliance, popularly referred as C.R.A is headed by The Great Team Kibet.

Mission

Our mission is to promote unity, peace, and prosperity in Krinyaga University and all other universities in Kenya through effective governance, social justice, and economic development.

Vision

Our vision is a Kenyan university that is democratic, inclusive, and equitable, where all comrades enjoy equal rights, opportunities, and dignity.
